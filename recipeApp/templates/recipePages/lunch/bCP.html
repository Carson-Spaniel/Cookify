Buffalo Chicken Paninis

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat your panini press or large skillet. Lightly grease with vegetable oil spray.


  2. In a bowl, toss grilled chicken with buffalo sauce; set aside.


  3. Take two slices of bread. Place sandwich under panini press for about 5 minutes or until golden.


  4. Slice in half and serve with your favorite chips.


  5. Enjoy!
