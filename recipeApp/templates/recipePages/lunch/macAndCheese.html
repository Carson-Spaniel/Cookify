Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.


  2. Preheat a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook noodles as the package says, strain and set aside.


  3. In a large pot on medium heat, add butter, garlic, and shallots. Cook for a couple of minutes until shallots are tender.


  4. Add flour and stir frequently until flour darkens and starts to bubble.


  5. Slowly add in milk and cream while stirring. Stir regularly until the mixture is thick and can coat the back of a spoon (about 10 minutes). Remove from heat.


  6. Add salt, pepper, paprika, cheddar, Gouda, and Gruyère. Mix until cheese is fully melted.


  7. Add noodles and chiles. Pour into a greased or buttered 8x8 dish.


  8. Top with shredded cheddar and asiago.


  9. Bake for 20 minutes until cheese is melted.


  10. Enjoy!
