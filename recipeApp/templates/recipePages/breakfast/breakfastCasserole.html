Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


  2. Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray.


  3. Combine the eggs, sour cream, milk, cheese, and salt and pepper in a large bowl. Mix on low speed with electric mixers, just until combined.


  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces with a wooden spoon as it cooks.


  5. Drain most of the grease and add the sausage to the bowl with the egg mixture.


  6. Add the bell peppers and onion to the same skillet the sausage was cooked in and sauté for 2-3 minutes.


  7. Add the sautéed bell peppers and onion to the bowl with the eggs and stir everything to combine.


  8. Pour the mixture into the greased 9x13 pan and bake for 35-50 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is just barely jiggly.


  9. Enjoy!
