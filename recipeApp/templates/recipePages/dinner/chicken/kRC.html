Mom's King Ranch Casserole

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan. Add bell pepper and onion. Cook over medium heat until tender.


  2. Blend in cream of chicken, rotel, and chopped chicken.


  3. Layer 4 tortillas in a 9x13 dish. Top with 1/3 of the tomato mixture, then 1/3 of the shredded cheese. Repeat 2 more times.


  4. Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes or until bubbling.


  5. Enjoy!
