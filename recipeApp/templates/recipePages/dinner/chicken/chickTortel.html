Instructions

  1. Cook chicken on medium heat seasoning with lemon pepper.


  2. Boil tortellini.


  3. Melt butter.


  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.


  5. Sprinkle flour in.


  6. Slowly whisk heavy cream in.


  7. Add salt and parmesan.


  8. Stir in spinach, tomatoes, and chicken.


  9. Enjoy!
