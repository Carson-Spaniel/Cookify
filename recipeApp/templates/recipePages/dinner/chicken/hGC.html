Emily's Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat two tablespoons of butter and add the diced chicken. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for 3-5 minutes on each side on medium to high heat to sear the chicken until it's no longer pink. Then remove the chicken from the pan.


  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, lemon juice, soy sauce, olive oil, crushed garlic, and red pepper flakes.


  3. Pour the honey garlic sauce over the cooked chicken and cook until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through.


  4. Serve the Honey Garlic Chicken over rice and with your favorite veggies for a delicious dinner any night of the week.


  5. Enjoy!
