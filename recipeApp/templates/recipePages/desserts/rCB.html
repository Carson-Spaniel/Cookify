Reese's Cheesecake Brownies

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Lightly coat a 9x13 pan with cooking spray.


  2. Prepare brownie batter according to its directions and pour batter into the 9x13 pan. Set aside.


  3. In a large bowl, beat softened cream cheese until light and fluffy. Add in sweetened condensed milk and peanut butter, and beat until smooth.


  4. Stir in the bag of Reese's Pieces candies. Spoon the cheesecake mixture over the brownie batter evenly.


  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until the cheesecake layer is set and the edges are golden brown.


  6. Let the brownies cool for 30 minutes, then refrigerate for another 30 minutes.


  7. In a microwave-safe bowl, add milk chocolate chips and whipping cream. Melt on high for 1 minute, checking and stirring throughout to avoid overcooking. Heat until melted and smooth.


  8. Pour and spread the melted chocolate evenly over the top of the brownies and cheesecake.


  9. Sprinkle the 12 large chopped Reese's Peanut Butter Cups over the top.


  10. Store covered in the refrigerator.


  11. Enjoy!
