Mom's Kitchen Sink Cookies

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees.


  2. In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, vanilla, and egg with a mixer on medium speed.


  3. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt (dough will be stiff).


  4. Stir in semi-sweet and milk chocolate chips, pretzels, and toffee.


  5. Drop 1/2 cup drop-fulls 4 inches apart, flatten slightly, and sprinkle a few salt flakes on top of each cookie.


  6. Bake for 13-16 minutes or until light brown around the edges; the center will be soft.


  7. Let cool for 5 minutes.


  8. Enjoy!
