Our Story

We first started talking to each other during high school tennis in 2019. I began to take different routes to my classes so that way I could see Emily. On December 23rd, 2019, is when we had our first date at The Trail of Lights in Austin. We both graduated in May of 2020 and began to look at where we were going to be going to school. By the end of the summer, I knew that I wanted to go to Texas Tech University for Computer Science. Emily wanted to go to Kansas University for Architecture. We began long distance when we both went to school. Long distance was and still is hard. We would get to see each other over Summer and Winter breaks for a little, as well as the occasional visit during a break in school. I created this website to allow us to stay connected and make different things easier.
