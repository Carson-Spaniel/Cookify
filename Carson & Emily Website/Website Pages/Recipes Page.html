Breakfast

Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork sausage
  • 12 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream (light or regular)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 green onions
  • 1/2 green bell pepper , diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper , diced
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray. Combine the eggs, sour cream, milk, cheese and salt and pepper in a large bowl. Mix on low speed with electric mixers, just until combined. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Drain most of the grease and add the sausage to the bowl with the egg mixture. Add the bell peppers and onion to the same skillet the sausage was cooked in and sauté for 2- 3 minutes. Add to the bowl with the eggs and stir everything to combine. Pour mixture into greased 9x13 pan and bake for 35-50 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is just barely jiggly. Leftover egg casserole can be stored in the fridge and enjoyed within 3-4 days. Leftovers are delicious reheated in the microwave.

Lunch

Mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb rigatoni
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar (extra for topping)
  • 1/2 cup smoked Gouda
  • 1/2 cup Gruyère
  • shredded asiago cheese for topping
  • 3tbsp all purpose flour
  • 3 1/2tbsp salted butter
  • 1 small diced shallot
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1cup milk
  • 1cup heavy cream
  • 1/2tsp white pepper
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can (4oz) diced green chiles

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Preheating a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook noodles as the package says, strain and set aside. In a large pot on medium heat add butter, garlic and shallots. Cook for a couple min until shallots are tender. Add flour and stir frequently until flour darkens and starts to bubble. Slowly add in milk and cream while stirring. Stir regularly until mixture is thick and can coat the back of a spoon (about 10 min). Remove from heat. Add salt, pepper, paprika, cheddar, Gouda, and Gruyère. Mix until cheese is fully melted. Add noodles and chiles. Pour into a greased or buttered 8x8 dish. Top with shredded cheddar and asiago. Bake for 20 min until cheese is melted.

Mom's King Ranch Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Medium Size Green Bell Pepper (Chopped)
  • 1 Medium Size Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cans Cream of Chicken
  • 2 Cans Rotel
  • 2-3 Cooked Chicken Breasts (Chopped)
  • 12 Corn Tortillas
  • 2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

Melt butter in large saucepan. Add bell pepper and onion. Cook over medium heat until tender. Blend in soup, rotel, and chicken. Layer 4 tortillas in a 9x13 dish. Top with 1/3 tomatoe mixture, then 1/3 cheese. Repeat 2 more times. Bake at 325 degrees for 40 minutes or until bubbling.

Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Chicken Breast (Diced)
  • 6-8 cloves of Garlic crushed
  • 1/4 cup of Honey
  • 1/4 cup of Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp. Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. Butter
  • Dash of Red pepper flakes
  • Sea Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

In a large pan heat two tablespoons of butter and add the chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook 3-5 minutes on each side on medium to high heat to sear the chicken and its no longer pink. Then remove from pan. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, lemon juice, soy sauce, olive oil and garlic. Pour over chicken and cook until sauce thickens and chicken is cooked through. Serve over rice and with your favorite veggies for a delicious dinner any night of the week.


Mendicino Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Fettuccince Pasta
  • 2 tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 6oz Italian Sausage, chopped (or 1 lb Ground meat)
  • 1/2 Onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp. Garlic, minced
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 4 cups Chicken Broth
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • [Optional] 4 tbsp. Basil, Chopped
  • [Optional] 1/2 cup White Wine

Instructions

Heat oil in a large saucepan over high heat. Add sausage, onion, garlic, and tomatoes and for saute 5 minutes. Add wine and chicken broth and bring to a boil. Add basil and uncooked pasta to the mixture. Reduce heat and simmer 4-6 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Kitchen Sink Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Butter (Softened)
  • 1 Cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 3/4 Cup Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 3/4 Cup Pretzels
  • 1/2 Toffee Bits
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Flaky Sea Salt

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, vanilla, and egg with mixer on medium speed. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt (dough will be stiff). Stir in semi sweet, milk chocolate chips, pretzels and toffee. Drop 1/2 cup dropfulls 4 inches apart, flatten slightly, sprinkle a few salt flakes on top of each cookie. Bake 13-16 minutes or until light brown around edges, center will be soft. Let cool for 5 minutes.


Reese's Cheesecake Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 Fudge Brownie Mix
  • 1 (8 oz) Package Cream Cheese
  • 1 (14 oz) Can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 (12 oz) Package Reese's Pieces
  • 1 (12 oz) Package Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 3 Tablespoon Whipping Cream
  • 12 Large Reese's Peanut Butter Cups (Chopped)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly coat 9x13 pan with cooking spray. Prepare brownie batter according to its directions and pour batter into the 9x13 pan and set aside. In a large bowl, beat softened cream cheese until light and fluffy. Add in sweetened condensed milk and peanut butter and beat until smooth. Stir in bag of Reese's Pieces candies. Spoon Cheesecake mixture over brownie batter evenly. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until cheesecake layer is set and edges are golden brown. Let cool for 30 minutes then refrigerate for 30 minutes. In a microwave safe bowl, add milk chocolatechips and whipping cream. Melt on high for 1 minute checking and stirring throughout so not to over cook. Heat unitl melted and smooth. Pour and spread evenly over the top of brownies/cheesecake. Sprinkle the 12 large chopped Reese's Peanut butter cups over top. Store covered in refrigerator.
